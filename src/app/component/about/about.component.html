Simulation Factors:

We compare each fighter by 6 different stats: Speed, Combat Ability, Intellegence, Durability, Avalible Powers, and Strength.
For fairness sake, the stat numbers range only between 0 and 100. Whomever is stronger in any given stat, obtains a point.
After all comparisons, the fighter with the most points wins! If the points end in a tie, the overall stats' numbers are added in total for to server as a tie-breaker.
